      Welcome to WordCraft!

      Craft words from the given letters and race against time. Here's how to play:

      1. You'll see 9 letter tiles on the screen.
      2. Form words using these letters. Each letter can be used only once per word.
      3. Words must be at least 2 letters long.
      4. Submit your word by typing it and pressing Enter or clicking the Submit button.
      5. Each valid word earns you points: 1 point for 2-letter words, and (length * (length - 2)) for longer words.
      6. Every correct word adds its length in seconds to your timer!
      7. Try to find the 9-letter word for maximum points!

      Example: Given letters A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I

      • "BAD" - 3 points, adds 3 seconds
      • "CHIEF" - 15 points, adds 5 seconds
      • "ACHIEVED" (9-letter word) - 49 points, adds 9 seconds
