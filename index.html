      Welcome to WordCraft!

      Craft as many words from the 9 letters on the screen.

      1. Use each letter once per word (min 2 letters).
      2. Type and submit words using Enter or the Submit button.
      3. Words must be at least 2 letters long.
      4. Each valid word adds its length in seconds to the timer.
      5. Find the 9-letter word for max points!

      Example: Given letters A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, I

      • "BAD" - 3 points, adds 3 seconds
      • "CHIEF" - 15 points, adds 5 seconds
      • "ACHIEVED" (9-letter word) - 49 points, adds 9 seconds
